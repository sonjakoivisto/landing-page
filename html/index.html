On my plate right now

Earth comes to live in the springtime. Green will take over the greyness of winter. Spring inspires to use the seasonal veggies like asparagus, peas, spinach and potatoes while cooking. Many are inclined to eat lighter meals like salads this time of the year but here are few delicious recipes that will get your belly filled.

My all-time favourite spring food is asparagus risotto. Asparagus is such a delicate veggie that it's only available this time of the year and its flavour matches perfectly to the smooth consistency of risotto. This Jamie Oliver recipe made by Gennaro Contaldo freshens up the usual asparagus risotto by adding peas and zuchhini to it.

Green curry is like fireworks of flavours. This Jamie Oliver's recipe will spice up the beautiful, sunny days coming up. It's super easy to alter to your own taste with using whatever you have in your fridge. Let these Thai flavours bring a smile to your face.
